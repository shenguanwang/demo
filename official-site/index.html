What importers usually confirm before placing an order.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Trial orders can start from 1-3 vehicles, subject to model availability, destination, and logistics feasibility.

What does the quotation include?

The formal quotation states the exact model, trim, vehicle price, China-side service scope, trade term, payment schedule, and validity period.

Can every model be registered in my country?

No assumption is made. Import certification, local registration, charging standard, language, and road-use requirements must be checked for the destination market.

How is the delivery schedule confirmed?

The schedule is confirmed after vehicle availability, payment, inspection, domestic transport, customs, and shipping capacity are reviewed.
